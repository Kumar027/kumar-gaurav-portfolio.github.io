Project Summary

This project analyzes and forecasts sales for Jaguar and Land Rover using historical wholesale and retail data. The goal was to understand long-term trends, model-level performance, seasonal patterns, and supply gaps to help optimize production, inventory, and future planning.

The analysis shows that sales grew steadily for more than a decade, peaked around year 10, and then declined sharply ‚Äì signaling major market challenges and demand shifts.

  • Detect oversupplied and undersupplied models

Major Insights

  • Discovery, Range Rover and RR Sport were top performers
  • Defender and some Jaguar SUVs showed inventory overstock
  • Q4 contributes ~26‚Äì27% of yearly sales, making it the critical demand period
